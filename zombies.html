Zombies
Mechanics - Characters - Planning - Story

A zombie apocalypse story written using All Flesh Must Be Eaten mechanics and random generators as a guide so that I can write it solo and still have an element of surprise within it.

It all started six months a go when a little-too-easy-going CDC employee caught the flu in the middle of the winter - no one thought anything of it, but because of that no one realized that it wasn't just a normal flu that he was coughing up.
