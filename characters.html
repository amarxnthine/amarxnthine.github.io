Qualities + Drawbacks

Qualities: 5 points

Drawbacks: 10 points, drawback points can be spent to add more qualities or skills on a 1-1 basis, or increase attributes at the cost of the level.

Skills

Skills cost 1 point per level from level 1-5, then cost 3 points per level aboe that. Special skills cost 2 points per level until level 5, and then 5 points per level after that.

Characters get 30 skill points to start with. If it is not listed here, it is assumed the character does not have that skill.
